# Find Fortune Route Product Context

## Register

Product UI. This is a professional stock strategy research and assisted decision workbench, not a marketing site.

## Users

- Individual or small-team investors researching China A-share opportunities.
- Users need fast scanning, explainable signals, risk context, and disciplined trade journaling.
- Users make the final decision; the product provides research, alerts, and learning samples.

## Product Purpose

Find Fortune Route helps users observe real A-share market data, compare strategy signals, inspect individual stocks, track virtual positions, and feed actual decisions back into a strategy-learning loop.

## Design Principles

- Calm confidence: the interface should feel serious, data-rich, and controlled.
- Decision first: every panel should help answer "what changed, why does it matter, what should I inspect next?"
- Evidence over decoration: visual emphasis must come from data hierarchy, charts, states, and interaction feedback.
- Fast scanning: dense information is acceptable when grouping, spacing, and labels are precise.
- No automatic trading language: signal wording should remain advisory and uncertainty-aware.

## Anti-References

- Do not make it look like a crypto landing page, generic AI dashboard, or colorful consumer finance app.
- Avoid oversized marketing hero sections, decorative cards inside cards, neon gradients, purple-blue glow themes, and meaningless abstract imagery.
- Avoid sparse layouts that waste screen area; this is a repeated-use research tool.

## Visual Direction

Professional financial terminal meets modern research notebook: compact panels, crisp separators, restrained palette, high-quality hover states, clear red/green market semantics for China A-share conventions, and blue only for navigation/action emphasis.
